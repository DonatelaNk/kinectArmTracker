NOTE: do not move the app without keeping the data/ folder relative to it!

App info:
- If a Kinect is plugged in the app will capture from the kinect. If not it will use a test video. 
- The armTracker only tracks the right arm of a person
- To track the left arm - flip the kinect image with the bFlipH toggle in the control panel
- The settings are dialed in for most users. Be careful modifying the settings. 
- There is a zipped backup of the settings file in data/ 
- Press 'p' to pause the video 
- Press 'g' to unpause the video
- Esc to exit the app.
